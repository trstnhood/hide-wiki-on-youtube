# Hide Wiki – Firefox Extension

---

## English

### What does Hide Wiki do?

Hide Wiki removes the Wikipedia and Britannica info panels that YouTube automatically displays below certain videos.

- Collects **no** data
- Requires **no** account
- Works **immediately** after installation
- Officially **verified and signed by Mozilla**

---

### Installation (3 simple steps)

#### Step 1: Open the file

Double-click the file:

**`hide-wiki-firefox-extension-1.0.0.xpi`**

*(If double-click doesn't work: Simply drag the file into an open Firefox window.)*

#### Step 2: Confirm

Firefox will show a message:

> **"Add Hide Wiki?"**

Click **"Add"**.

#### Step 3: Done!

That's it. Hide Wiki is now active. Go to YouTube and the Wikipedia panels are gone.

---

### Does it work?

Here's how to test it:

1. Go to **youtube.com**
2. Search for a topic like **"COVID-19"** or **"climate change"**
3. The Wikipedia or Britannica info panel below the video should **no longer appear**

---

### Uninstall

If you want to remove the extension:

1. Type in the Firefox address bar: **about:addons** and press Enter
2. Find **"Hide Wiki"** in the list
3. Click the three dots (**...**) next to it
4. Select **"Remove"**

---

### FAQ

**Is this safe?**
Yes. The extension has been reviewed and signed by Mozilla. It collects no data and does not communicate with the internet. Everything runs locally in your browser.

**Does it work on mobile?**
No, only on Firefox for Desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux).

**Do I need to restart Firefox?**
No, the extension works immediately after installation.

**The panels are still there?**
Reload the YouTube page (F5 or Ctrl+R). If it still doesn't work, check under about:addons whether Hide Wiki is enabled.
